# Methodology

This independent simulator studies hybrid RF and optical wireless communication (OWC)
orchestration using synthetic traces.

The pipeline creates fictional users, applications, mobility conditions, RF interference,
OWC line-of-sight blockage, beam-alignment error, and traffic demand. It estimates RF and
OWC link quality, adds simple rolling predictions, evaluates several orchestration policies,
and compares throughput, latency, reliability, energy cost, OWC utilization, handover rate,
and service-satisfaction proxies.

The methodology is intentionally transparent and reproducible. It is designed for research
experimentation, not direct infrastructure control.
